Help: Attachments and Volumes

New attachment: Give it a name and add a description! It will appear under the /v/attachments/<foldername>, therefore it is restricted to alphanumerical characters only.

List attachment: List of all the existing attachments with their foldername and their description. Use the search bar on the top of the page to filter it.

List volumes: List of all the volumes you have access to. Use the search bar on the top of the page to filter it.
