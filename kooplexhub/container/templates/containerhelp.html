Help: Environments

New environment: Add a human readable friendly name and choose an image for the environment. Use the dropdown in the bottom to see image descriptions!

List environment: This section shows us the list of all our containers. Use the search bar on the top to filter the list.

Click on the green down pointing arrow to open the dropdown panel for an environment. Small icons show the attached or mounted projects and attachments/volumes, below that warnings and container status messages.

The buttons on the bottom are the following: Launch or access an environment, restatrt, stop the environment, refresh status message and configure environment. Bottom right button is for deleting the environment.

Configure environment - Name/Image: update the friendly name or the image of the container.

Configure environment - Projects: add projects to an environment. They will appear under the folder /v/projects/.

Configure environment - Courses: add courses and manage course material in an environment, access student working directories and submitted notebooks. They will appear under the folder /v/course/.

Configure environment - Attachments: mount any attachments into an environment. They will appear under the folder /v/attachments/ .

Configure environment - Volumes: mount any volume into an environment. They will appear under the folder /v/volumes/ .

List report environment: Lists containers that run the necessary processes for reports. Use the search bar on the top of to filter the page.
