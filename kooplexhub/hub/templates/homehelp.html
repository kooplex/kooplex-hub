 Help: Home page

This is the Kooplex home screen. Note on the bottom informs you about the status of the platform. In the top-right corner there is Help, Messages and Profile: Acess your profile, documentation and get to know more about our work at Kooplex.

Messages appear in the lower right corner informing you about the success or failure of your last operations.

Environment: Create a new Environment (virtual machine), configure/launch etc. your notebook and report Environments.

Project: Adds collaborative features to Environments.

Report: You can publish static or interactive Reports for a larger audience.

Education: Add course features to Environments and create/handle Assignments.

Storage: Create or list Attachments and configure data Volumes.
